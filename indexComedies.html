COMEDIES

Paul Blart: Mall Cop (2009)  |  Rated: PG

Paul Blart (Kevin James) is a low-key security guard that works at a New Jersey mall. His dream is to become a cop. However, Blart always fails the physical exam due to his being overweight. When a gang of skilled criminals take hostages within the mall, Blart becomes trapped inside the mall. He thus becomes the police department's contact person on the inside and attempts to bring the criminals to justice on his own. This is Blart's chance to show everyone involved that he's a capable lawman, in spite of his weight.

Stars: Kevin James, Keir O'Donnell, Jayma Mays
Writers: Kevin James, Nick Bakay
Director: Steve Carr
Genre: Comedy


Think like a Man (2012)   |  Rated: PG-13

The movie is based on Actor-turned-relationship expert Steve Harvey's New York Times bestselling book - Act Like a Lady, Think Like a Man. Four women - Gabrielle Union, Meagan Good, Regina Hall and Taraji P. Henson - have devised a plan to get their men - Michael Ealey, Jerry Ferrara, Terrence J, and Romany Malco - to abide by the women's every wish. Once implemented the plan works very well. However, the men eventually discover what the women have been doing, and at that point it becomes an all-out war between the sexes.

Stars: Chris Brown, Gabrielle Union, Kevin Hart
Writers: Keith Merryman (screenplay), David A. Newman (screenplay),
Steve Harvey (book, Act Like a Lady, Think Like a Man)
Director: Tim Story
Genre: Comedy
