HORROR MOVIES

Amityville Horror (1979)  |  Rated: R

Amityville Horror is based on a true account from newlyweds George (James Brolin) and Kathy Lutz (Margot Kidder) and their three children. The Lutz family moved into what they believed would be their dream home and quickly find out it's not the case. The family purchases the home anyway after learning about its dark history from the real estate agent. George Lutz states that "Houses do not have memories." However, they do have an exorcism performed on the property prior to the "move-in" date. Unfortunately, the exorcism does not work, and the evil spirits in the house affect the family's health. George Lutz temporarily loses his eyesight and becomes very ill. The Lutz family tries to stay in the house until their daughter starts to see people, aka ghosts. When their daughter sees the ghosts, they decide that the Amityville house is a loss and the Lutzes move out, never to return.

Stars: James Brolin, Margot Kidder, Rod Steiger
Writers: Sandor Stern (screenplay), Jay Anson (novel)
Director: Stuart Rosenberg
Genre: Horror Movie
